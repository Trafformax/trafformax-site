This Cookie Policy explains how TrafforMax uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function, remember your preferences, and measure performance. Similar technologies include pixels, tags and local storage.

2. Types of cookies we use

Strictly necessary

Required for the website to function (for example, remembering your region choice). These cannot be switched off in our systems.

Performance & analytics

Help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it (for example, Google Analytics).

Functional

Remember choices you make (such as language or region) to personalise your experience.

Marketing & advertising

Set by us or advertising partners to measure and optimise campaigns and, where permitted, show relevant ads (for example, the Meta Pixel, Google Ads, Microsoft, TikTok).

4. Managing your cookies

When required by law we ask for your consent to non-essential cookies via a cookie banner. You can change your preferences at any time and can also control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect how the site works.

5. Third-party cookies

Some cookies are set by third parties (such as advertising and analytics providers). Their use of data is governed by their own privacy and cookie policies.
