FAQ

Questions, answered

Are your leads exclusive?+

Yes. Every lead is delivered to a single contractor and is never shared or resold.

What information comes with each lead?+

Every lead includes the homeowner’s contact details, property information, project type, budget, timeline, and qualification data collected during the enquiry process.

How are leads delivered?+

Leads are delivered instantly via CRM, email, webhook, API, or other integrations — depending on your setup.

What happens if a lead is invalid?+

Flag it — wrong area, fake number, renter or duplicate is reviewed and credited or replaced at no charge.

Can I choose specific regions?+

Yes. You define your exact ZIP and service-area coverage, plus daily caps.

How many leads can I receive per month?+

From a handful to thousands — set a cap around your team’s capacity.

Do you also offer media buying services?+

Yes. We can manage paid acquisition for your own brand, or generate exclusive leads through our in-house campaigns.
