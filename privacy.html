1. Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Contact details — name, business name, email, phone number.
  • Enquiry details — service required, target markets, budget, volumes and other information you submit through our forms or quiz.
  • Communications — messages you send us by email, chat or messaging apps (WhatsApp, Telegram).

Information collected automatically

  • Device and usage data — IP address, browser type, pages viewed, referring URLs.
  • Cookies and similar technologies — see our Cookie Policy.

Information from third parties

  • Advertising and analytics platforms (e.g. Meta, Google, Microsoft, TikTok) that help us measure and deliver campaigns.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide, operate and improve our services, including generating, verifying and delivering leads.
  • To respond to your enquiries and provide customer support.
  • To send you service updates and, where permitted, marketing communications.
  • To measure and optimise advertising campaigns.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

3. Legal bases (UK/EU GDPR)

  • Consent — for certain marketing communications and non-essential cookies.
  • Legitimate interests — to run and improve our business and services.
  • Contract — to provide services you have requested.
  • Legal obligation — where we are required to process data by law.

4. How we share information

  • Contractors / clients — where you are a homeowner who submitted an enquiry, your details may be delivered to a vetted contractor or client to provide the quote you requested.
  • Service providers — CRM, hosting, analytics, communications and payment providers acting on our instructions.
  • Advertising platforms — in aggregated or hashed form for campaign measurement.
  • Legal / safety — where required by law or to protect our rights.

We do not sell personal information for money.

5. Marketing & consent

Where you opt in, we (or the contractor you were matched with) may contact you by phone, SMS or email about your enquiry. You can withdraw consent or opt out at any time via the unsubscribe link, by replying STOP to messages, or by emailing us. US recipients: our contact practices are intended to comply with the TCPA; consent is not a condition of purchase.

We operate across the UK, EU and USA. Where personal data is transferred internationally, we use appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses or the UK IDTA.

7. Data retention

We keep personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described above and to meet legal, accounting or reporting requirements, after which it is deleted or anonymised.

To exercise any right, contact privacy@trafformax.com.
